To the Most Amazing Person,

As I write this letter, my heart beats with every word...

I still remember the day I first saw you in the classroom, wearing that black mask.
From my bench, I was lost in your beautiful eyes, wondering how someone could be this breathtaking.
I couldn't help but think about the kind of person who would be lucky enough to have you by their side.

Little did I know, fate had something incredible planned for me,
that lucky person turned out to be me. And I can't thank fate enough for making our paths cross.

From that first moment to now, you have been nothing short of amazing.
Your kindness, your laughter, your presence, all of it makes my world brighter.
I appreciate every little thing you do, from the way you support me to the way you simply exist and make life more beautiful.

Thank you for being you. Thank you for being here. And most of all, thank you for letting me be the lucky guy who gets to share this journey with you.
